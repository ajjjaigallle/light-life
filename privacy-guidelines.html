Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 23, 2025

Lighter DEX ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website app-lighter-gamma.vercel.app and use our online learning platform services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you:

Register for an account: name, email address, password, country, and educational background

Enroll in workshops: course selections, learning preferences, and payment information

Complete assignments: submitted work, project files, and interactive exercise responses

Communicate with us: messages sent through contact forms, support requests, or email

Participate in forums: comments, posts, and collaborative tool interactions

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you access our platform, we automatically collect:

Device information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers

Usage data: pages viewed, time spent on pages, links clicked, and navigation paths

Learning analytics: course progress, completion rates, quiz scores, and engagement metrics

Cookies and tracking technologies: session data, preferences, and authentication tokens

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

Payment processors: transaction confirmation and payment status

Authentication services: when you use single sign-on options

Educational partners: enrollment verification and credential information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Service Delivery

Provide access to workshops, assignments, and learning materials

Process enrollments and manage your account

Deliver interactive exercises and collaborative tools

Track progress and issue certificates upon course completion

2.2 Platform Improvement

Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

Develop new features and improve existing functionality

Optimize content based on learner engagement

Test and troubleshoot technical issues

2.3 Communication

Send course updates and assignment notifications

Provide customer support and respond to inquiries

Share educational content and workshop announcements

Deliver administrative messages about policy changes or service updates

2.4 Legal and Security

Prevent fraud and unauthorized access

Enforce terms of service and acceptable use policies

Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests

Protect rights and safety of users and staff

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We share information with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, including:

Hosting and infrastructure providers

Payment processors and billing services

Email delivery and communication platforms

Analytics and performance monitoring tools

Customer support software providers

3.2 Educational Partners

With your consent, we may share completion certificates, achievement data, or credentials with educational institutions or employers.

3.3 Collaborative Features

When you participate in forums, group projects, or collaborative exercises, your contributions may be visible to other enrolled participants.

3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or when necessary to protect rights, safety, or property.

3.5 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

Encryption: data transmitted over secure connections using industry-standard protocols

Access controls: restricted access to personal information on a need-to-know basis

Regular audits: security assessments and vulnerability testing

Secure storage: protected databases with backup and recovery procedures

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

Provide services and maintain your account

Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes

Enforce agreements and prevent fraud

Maintain learning records and issue credentials

When information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it. Course completion records and certificates may be retained indefinitely for verification purposes.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Correction

You may access, update, or correct your account information through your profile settings or by contacting us at info@app-lighter-gamma.vercel.app.

6.2 Data Portability

You may request a copy of your personal information in a structured, commonly used format.

6.3 Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and personal information, subject to legal retention requirements and legitimate business needs.

6.4 Marketing Communications

You may opt out of promotional emails by using the unsubscribe link in messages or updating your communication preferences. Transactional emails related to your account and enrollments cannot be disabled.

6.5 Cookies

You may control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

8. International Data Transfers

As a transnational learning platform, we may transfer and process information in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to external websites, resources, or services not operated by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

10. Blockchain Technology

Our platform may utilize blockchain technology for certain educational applications, including credential verification and learning records. Information recorded on blockchain systems may be permanent and publicly accessible by design. We will clearly inform you before any information is recorded on a blockchain.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or business operations. We will notify you of material changes by:

Posting the updated policy on this page with a new "Last Updated" date

Sending email notifications to registered users

Displaying a prominent notice on our platform

Your continued use of our services after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
