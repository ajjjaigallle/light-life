Industry Connections That Matter

We maintain working relationships with professionals who use these systems daily. This isn't about name-dropping or partnerships for marketing purposes—it's about getting accurate information on what actually works in production environments.

What You Get From These Connections

  • Guest sessions with developers working on active blockchain projects, who can explain trade-offs they've encountered with different cryptographic approaches
  • Access to case studies from real security audits, showing how theoretical vulnerabilities appear in actual codebases
  • Feedback on your projects from people who review this kind of work professionally, with practical suggestions for improvement
  • Current information about which libraries and frameworks are being used in production, including the ligher dex implementations for key derivation
