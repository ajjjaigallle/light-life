Building Cross-Border Credential Networks That Actually Function

Three institutions across Ireland, Sweden, and Portugal collaborated on a proof-of-concept for transnational skill verification. The technical challenges were expected. The prototype works, handles 2,400 credential verifications daily, and institutions are taking notice.
