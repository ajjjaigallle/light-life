Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 23, 2025

Lighter DEX ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our educational platform and services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

Name, email address, phone number, postal address, date of birth, profile photograph, payment information, educational background and qualifications, course enrollment and completion data, learning preferences and goals, communication preferences, and any other information you choose to provide.

Automatically Collected Information

When you access our platform, we automatically collect certain information, including:

IP address, browser type and version, device identifiers, operating system, pages viewed and time spent on pages, referring website addresses, clickstream data, cookies and similar tracking technologies, session recordings and heatmaps, bandwidth and connection speed, and error logs and diagnostic data.

Educational Data

We collect information related to your learning activities:

Course progress and completion status, quiz and assignment scores, participation in group sessions, interaction with instructors and peers, video conference attendance and duration, learning materials accessed, study time and patterns, and feedback and evaluations.

Communications

We retain records of communications between you and us, including support tickets, email correspondence, chat messages with instructors, forum posts and discussions, and video conference recordings when applicable.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Service Delivery

To provide, maintain, and improve our educational services, process your enrollment and payments, deliver course content and materials, facilitate live sessions with instructors, enable peer collaboration and group learning, track your progress and achievements, and provide technical support.

Personalization

To create personalized learning paths, recommend relevant courses and content, adapt difficulty levels based on performance, match you with appropriate instructors, suggest study schedules and materials, and customize your user experience.

Communication

To send course-related notifications, provide administrative updates, respond to inquiries and support requests, deliver marketing communications with your consent, send newsletters and educational content, and notify you of platform changes or new features.

Analytics and Improvement

To analyze platform usage and engagement, monitor educational outcomes and effectiveness, identify technical issues and bugs, conduct research and development, improve course quality and content, and optimize user experience.

Legal and Security

To comply with legal obligations, enforce our terms and conditions, protect against fraud and abuse, secure our platform and systems, resolve disputes, and protect our rights and property.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Instructors and Educational Staff

We share relevant information with instructors to facilitate your learning, including your name, progress data, assignment submissions, and attendance records.

Other Learners

In group sessions and collaborative environments, your name, profile information, and contributions may be visible to other participants.

Service Providers

We engage third-party service providers to perform functions on our behalf, including payment processing, video conferencing, email delivery, data storage and hosting, analytics services, customer support tools, and content delivery networks.

Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, sale of assets, or bankruptcy, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, in response to legal processes, to protect our rights and property, to prevent fraud or security threats, or in emergency situations affecting safety.

With Your Consent

We may share information for other purposes with your explicit consent or at your direction.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Account information is retained while your account is active. Educational records and course data are retained for a minimum of seven years after course completion. Payment records are retained as required by financial regulations. Communications and support tickets are retained for three years. Automated logs and analytics data are typically retained for two years.

You may request deletion of your data subject to legal and legitimate business requirements.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

Encryption of data in transit and at rest, secure socket layer technology, regular security assessments and audits, access controls and authentication, employee training on data protection, incident response procedures, and regular backup systems.

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Correction

You may access, review, and update your personal information through your account settings or by contacting us.

Data Portability

You may request a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used format.

Deletion

You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions for legal compliance and legitimate business purposes.

Marketing Communications

You may opt out of receiving promotional communications by using the unsubscribe mechanism in our emails or updating your communication preferences.

Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality.

Account Deactivation

You may deactivate your account at any time by contacting our support team.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance your experience and collect usage data.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality, including authentication, security, and session management.

Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our platform through analytics and diagnostics.

Functional Cookies: Enable enhanced features and personalization based on your preferences.

Targeting Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie consent tool.

Third-Party Links and Services

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites, applications, or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any information.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for users aged sixteen and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under sixteen without parental consent. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

For users between sixteen and eighteen, we recommend parental involvement in the learning process.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on our platform and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your continued use of our services after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
