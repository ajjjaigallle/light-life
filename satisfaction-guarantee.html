Refund Policy

Last Updated: July 21, 2025

This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which Lighter DEX ("we," "us," or "our") provides refunds for services purchased through our educational platform at app-lighter-gamma.vercel.app.

General Refund Principles

We are committed to providing high-quality educational services. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we offer refunds under the conditions outlined in this policy. All refund requests are subject to review and approval based on the circumstances of each case.

Eligibility for Refunds

Individual Online Sessions

Refunds for individual online sessions may be requested under the following conditions:

    Cancellation Notice: You must provide at least 24 hours notice before the scheduled session start time to be eligible for a full refund.

    Technical Issues: If technical difficulties on our platform prevent you from attending a session, and we are unable to resolve the issue or reschedule, you will receive a full refund.

    Instructor Cancellation: If an instructor cancels a session and no suitable alternative time can be arranged, you will receive a full refund.

Group Online Sessions

Refunds for group online sessions are subject to the following terms:

    Early Cancellation: Full refunds are available if you cancel at least 48 hours before the first scheduled group session.

    Partial Attendance: If you attend fewer than two sessions of a multi-session group course, you may request a partial refund for the remaining sessions, minus any completed sessions and a processing fee.

    Course Discontinuation: If we discontinue or cancel a group course, you will receive a full refund for any unpaid sessions or a pro-rated refund based on sessions not yet delivered.

Learning Path Subscriptions

For personalized learning path subscriptions:

    Trial Period: We offer a trial period during which you may cancel and receive a full refund if you are not satisfied with the service. The duration of the trial period will be clearly communicated at the time of purchase.

    Monthly Subscriptions: Monthly subscriptions may be cancelled at any time, with access continuing until the end of the current billing period. No partial refunds are provided for monthly subscriptions.

    Annual Subscriptions: Annual subscriptions may be eligible for a pro-rated refund if cancelled within the first 30 days of purchase. After 30 days, no refunds will be provided, but you may cancel to prevent future billing.

Course Materials and Digital Products

Digital course materials, downloadable content, and digital products are generally non-refundable once accessed or downloaded. Exceptions may be made if:

    The materials are significantly different from what was described or advertised.

    Technical issues prevent you from accessing the materials, and we cannot resolve the problem.

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

    Sessions or courses that have been fully completed.

    Services where you failed to attend without providing adequate notice as specified above.

    Promotional or discounted services explicitly marked as non-refundable at the time of purchase.

    Administrative or processing fees associated with enrollment or subscription management.

Refund Request Process

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

    Contact Us: Send an email to info@app-lighter-gamma.vercel.app with your refund request. Include your full name, contact information, order or transaction number, and a detailed explanation of your reason for requesting a refund.

    Review Period: We will review your request within 5 to 7 business days and notify you of our decision via email.

    Approval and Processing: If your refund is approved, we will process the refund to your original payment method within 10 to 14 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account may vary depending on your financial institution.

Required Information

To facilitate the refund process, please provide:

    Proof of purchase or transaction receipt.

    Details of the service or product for which you are requesting a refund.

    Documentation of any technical issues or other circumstances supporting your request.

Processing Time and Method

Approved refunds will be processed using the same payment method used for the original purchase. Please note:

    Credit card refunds may take 5 to 10 business days to appear on your statement, depending on your card issuer.

    Bank transfer refunds may take up to 14 business days.

    Payment platform refunds (such as through third-party payment processors) will follow the refund timeline of that platform.

Exceptional Circumstances

We understand that unforeseen circumstances may arise. In cases of serious illness, family emergencies, or other extenuating situations, we may consider refund requests outside the standard policy terms. Please contact us directly to discuss your situation, and we will work with you to find a fair resolution.

Modifications and Rescheduling

As an alternative to requesting a refund, you may be able to:

    Reschedule Sessions: Individual and some group sessions may be rescheduled to a more convenient time, subject to instructor availability.

    Transfer Credits: In certain cases, credits from cancelled sessions may be applied toward future services or alternative courses.

    Pause Subscriptions: Subscription services may offer the option to pause your subscription temporarily rather than cancelling.

Please contact us at info@app-lighter-gamma.vercel.app to explore these options.

Disputes and Chargebacks

If you have a concern about a charge or service, we encourage you to contact us directly before initiating a chargeback with your financial institution. We are committed to resolving issues fairly and promptly.

Initiating a chargeback without first attempting to resolve the issue with us may result in suspension of your account and access to our services pending resolution of the dispute.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify or update this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our refund terms.

Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
